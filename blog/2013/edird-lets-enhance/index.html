EDIRD: Let's Enhance 🐯

I spend a lot of time tinkering with computers, it’s one of the big themes in my life. I have a pretty well-grounded idea of what computers are capable of and what they’re not—which is why this compilation of people treating computers as magic entertains me so much. Of course as a writer I also appreciate that sometimes you just need magic to get the characters from point A to point B, so I don’t hold it against them. Also, props to the author for assembling these clips with expertise and transformative execution: the new work speaks in a way that the components didn’t. Well done.
